About Us

Proggeto CinquantUno provides an unique solution to consumers

such as home-buyers, financial institutions, government or investors,

to review risks regarding natural disasters and UFO sightings in a determined location.

People looking to purchase a home or property in the United States, or otherwise interested

in a risk assessment of a piece of land, will all benefit from this project.
